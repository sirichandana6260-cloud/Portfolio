Safety Tips

Small steps that make a big difference

Always keep your phone charged

A charged phone allows you to quickly call emergency services and share your location.

Avoid walking alone at night

Stay in well-lit areas and try to travel with friends or trusted people.

Share your location with trusted people

Location sharing helps family or friends track your safety during travel.

Speak up if something feels wrong

Trust your instincts and seek help immediately if you feel unsafe.

Save emergency numbers

Keep important contacts like police, family, or trusted friends saved for emergencies.
